English Proficiency:

\n

Danag guides you through the English proficiency requirements, whether by holding a valid passport from a specific country or providing evidence of successful completion of recognized English language tests. This includes achieving an IELTS overall score of at least 6 with no band less than 5, an OET score of at least 'B' in each component, a TOEFL iBT total score of at least 64, a PTE Academic overall test score of at least 50 with a minimum score of 36 in each component, or a Cambridge English: Advanced (CAE) overall test score of at least 169 with a minimum score of at least 154 in each of the four test components.

\n

Australian Study Requirement:

\n

Danag assists in ensuring that applicants meet the two years study requirement and complete eligible qualifications within six months of applying for the visa, a crucial step in the process.

\n

Health and Character Requirements:

\n

Our team provides comprehensive guidance on the medical examination, AFP Certificate for character requirements, and the necessity of health insurance, including evidence of applying for an Australian Federal Police check within the 12 months before the visa application.

\n

Reciprocal Health Care Agreements:

\n

Danag ensures that applicants provide evidence of adequate health insurance unless their country has a reciprocal health care agreement with Australia, streamlining the process and ensuring a hassle-free application.
